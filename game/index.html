Welcome to Guess the Number!

Can you find the secret number?

Dive into our simple yet intriguing game where your goal is to guess a hidden number between 1 and 100. You have 10 tries to find the number, and each attempt gets you closer to becoming a master guesser!

How to Play:

  • Start the Game: Click the link below to begin your guessing journey.
  • Enter Your Guess: Type in a number between 1 and 100 and submit it.
  • Use the Hints: After each guess, you'll receive a hint telling you whether the secret number is higher or lower than your guess.
  • Keep Guessing: You have up to 10 tries to find the number. Can you do it in fewer?
  • Check the Leaderboard: After you finish, see how you stack up against other players on the leaderboard. Aim for the top with the fewest guesses!
